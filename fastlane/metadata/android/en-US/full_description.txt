Do you like Video Game Music (aka VGM)? If so, you came to the right place!

Listen to several of the awesomest videogame songs from your favorite video games directly from your device. This app is based on the VIP Aersia playlist (nowadays VIPVGM), which contains the very best VGM tracks as curated by Cats777.

The original playlist is available at <a href="https://www.vipvgm.net/">https://www.vipvgm.net/</a>. All Tracks © & ℗ Their Respective Owners.

Vidya Music is written in Jetpack Compose and is FLOSS software. The source code is available at <a href="https://github.com/MateusRodCosta/VidyaMusic">https://github.com/MateusRodCosta/VidyaMusic</a> and licensed under AGPLv3+.
